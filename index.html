About

A barbershop built on precision and personalized service.

The Straight Edge Barbershop combines modern barbering with a welcoming hometown atmosphere. We offer a comprehensive range of services, including classic haircuts, skin fades, detailed fades, and braids, catering to all hair textures. Conveniently located near downtown Tahlequah, we excel in contemporary styles, beard trims, and fades, ensuring a tailored experience for every client. Our commitment to precision and personalized service has earned us the distinction of being Tahlequah’s #1 Barbershop for eight consecutive years. Experience the perfect blend of style and comfort at The Straight Edge Barbershop.
